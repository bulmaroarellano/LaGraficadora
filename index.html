Instructions

To graph a pair of coordinates in the (x, y) plane do the following:

  • Insert a pair of coordinates (valid numerical values) in the fields with the legend Point X and Point Y, as appropriate.
  • Click on the button labeled "DRAW IT!".

    • Considerations:

  • The coordinates must be within the range of 1 to 800 for the X axis and between 1 and 300 for the Y axis.
  • Any non-numerical data or data outside the established ranges mentioned in point 1 will prevent the correct operation of the algorithm.
  • If you want to restart and insert a pair of new coordinates then click on the "CLEAR" button
